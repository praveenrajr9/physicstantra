Book 1

The Tiny Ant Who Outsmart Them All

Topic: Newton's Laws of Motion Hero: Nia the Ant Ages: 8-12

When the animals of Willow Woods announce their Great Race, nobody expects tiny Nia the Ant to enter. But Nia has a secret weapon — she understands the laws of motion! Through four thrilling parts, young readers discover Newton’s Three Laws as Nia uses inertia, force, and action-reaction to overcome every obstacle and prove that brains beat brawn.

What you’ll learn:

  • Newton’s First Law (Inertia) — why objects stay still or keep moving
  • Newton’s Second Law (F=ma) — how force and mass determine acceleration
  • Newton’s Third Law (Action-Reaction) — every push has an equal push back
  • How these laws work together in the real world

Each chapter includes a Science Spot with real-world examples and fun experiments you can try at home!
