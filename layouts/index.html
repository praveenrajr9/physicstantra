What is Physicstantra?

Inspired by the timeless Panchatantra, which taught wisdom through animal tales, Physics Tantra teaches physics the same way — through stories children actually want to read.

Each book follows a curious animal hero on an adventure that brings one big physics idea to life. No formulas to memorize. No textbooks to dread. Just vivid characters, real science, and the kind of storytelling that makes difficult concepts feel effortless.

From gravity to quantum mechanics, from light to spacetime — children absorb deep physics naturally, the way they absorb a good story: without even realizing they're learning.
