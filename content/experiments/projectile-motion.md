## How It Works

Adjust the launch angle and initial velocity, then fire! Watch the projectile trace a parabolic path under the influence of gravity.

**Try this:**
- What angle gives the maximum range? (Hint: it's 45°)
- What happens at very steep angles?
- How does doubling the velocity affect the range?

**The Physics:** Projectile motion combines constant horizontal velocity with vertical acceleration due to gravity (9.8 m/s²). The result is always a parabola.
