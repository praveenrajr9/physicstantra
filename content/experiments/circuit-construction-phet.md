## About This Simulation

Grab batteries, resistors, light bulbs, and wires to build circuits. Use the ammeter and voltmeter to measure current and voltage. See what happens when you connect bulbs in series vs parallel.

**Key concepts:** Electric current, voltage, resistance, Ohm's Law, series and parallel circuits
