Echo the Dolphin can "see" with sound — but how? In this underwater adventure, Echo and friends explore the physics of sound waves, from the vibrating strings of a guitar to the sonar pulses that map the ocean floor. Discover how sound travels, why we hear echoes, and how music is really just math in disguise.

**What you'll learn:**
- Sound as mechanical waves — frequency, amplitude, wavelength
- How sound travels through different materials
- Resonance, harmonics, and the physics of music
- Echolocation, sonar, and ultrasound technology
