Lumi the Firefly has always been proud of her glow, but she never understood *how* it works. Guided by curiosity, Lumi explores the electromagnetic spectrum, discovers why the sky is blue, how rainbows form, and why light behaves as both a wave and a particle.

**What you'll learn:**
- The nature of light — waves and photons
- Reflection, refraction, and diffraction
- The electromagnetic spectrum and visible light
- Wave-particle duality — light's biggest mystery
