What makes things move? Zuki the Zebra has always wondered. When a mysterious challenge forces her to figure out how energy works, Zuki embarks on a thrilling adventure through the physics of motion, work, and power — discovering that the secret to making things move was inside her all along.

**What you'll learn:**
- Forms of energy — kinetic, potential, thermal, and more
- Work, power, and mechanical advantage
- Conservation of energy — the universe's golden rule
- How energy transforms and drives everything that moves
