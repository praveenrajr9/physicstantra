Zuki the Zebra is always busy but never seems to get anything done. When a wise tortoise teaches her about energy, work, and the nature of time itself, Zuki realizes that physics holds the key to efficiency — and to understanding the universe.

**What you'll learn:**
- Forms of energy — kinetic, potential, thermal, and more
- Work, power, and mechanical advantage
- Conservation of energy — the universe's golden rule
- The nature of time and how it connects to energy
