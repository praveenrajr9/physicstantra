Tempo the Cheetah is the fastest animal alive — but what would happen if she could run even faster? At near-light speeds, time slows down, distances shrink, and mass increases. Einstein's theories of relativity reveal a universe where space and time are woven into a single fabric, and Tempo is about to experience it firsthand.

**What you'll learn:**
- Special Relativity — time dilation and length contraction
- The speed of light as the cosmic speed limit
- General Relativity — gravity as curved spacetime
- Spacetime, wormholes, and the nature of the universe
