What if you could shrink down to the size of an atom? Atom the Mouse does exactly that — and discovers a world stranger than any fantasy. In the quantum realm, particles tunnel through walls, exist in two states at once, and are connected across vast distances. This mind-bending adventure makes quantum physics accessible and unforgettable.

**What you'll learn:**
- Atomic structure — protons, neutrons, electrons
- Quantum mechanics basics — superposition and uncertainty
- Wave-particle duality at the atomic scale
- Quantum entanglement and the future of quantum computing
