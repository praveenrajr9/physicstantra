Pip the Penguin is full of questions — Why is the sky blue? Why does ice float? Why do stars twinkle? When he builds a magical Question Machine, Pip learns that every great discovery starts with a simple question. Follow Pip on an adventure through the scientific method, where curiosity leads to experiments, and experiments lead to answers.

**What you'll learn:**
- The scientific method — observation, hypothesis, experiment, conclusion
- How to ask great scientific questions
- Why curiosity is a scientist's superpower
- Real experiments you can try at home

Available as a **paperback** — perfect for young explorers who love holding a real book!
