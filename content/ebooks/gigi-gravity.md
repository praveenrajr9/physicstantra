Why do things fall down and not up? Gigi the Giraffe has always wondered, and with Professor Hoot the owl as her guide, she's about to find out. From Newton's apple to Einstein's curved spacetime, from black holes to astronaut adventures — this book takes young readers on a gravity-defying journey through one of nature's most fundamental forces.

**What you'll learn:**
- Why things fall — the basics of gravitational attraction
- Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation
- Einstein's revolutionary idea: gravity as curved spacetime
- Orbits, black holes, and the future of space travel

Featuring beloved characters Bounce the kangaroo, Milo the mouse, and Captain Koko the astronaut monkey!
