A mysterious blackout threatens Willow Woods, and Nia the Ant must journey to understand the invisible forces of electricity and magnetism. From static shocks to electromagnetic waves, Nia discovers that these two forces are really one — and that understanding them could save everything she loves.

**What you'll learn:**
- Electric charge, current, and circuits
- Magnetism and magnetic fields
- How electricity and magnetism are connected
- Electromagnetic waves — from radio to light to X-rays
